## Context

Khora, a fast-growing EdTech startup after their Series A, needed a Design System to accelerate product development and maintain visual consistency.

## Research

Existing system audit, interviews with design and development teams, benchmark of market best practices.

## Process

Creation of a modular system based on design tokens, documented on Storybook and integrated into Figma.

## Solution

A complete Design System with over 120 components, usage guidelines, and comprehensive documentation for teams.

## Results

Development time for new features was reduced by 40% and visual consistency was maintained across all products.
