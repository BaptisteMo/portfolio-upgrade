## Context

Nexus CRM is a customer relationship management tool used by over 500 companies. The aging interface was causing frustrations and productivity loss.

## Research

Observation sessions with 20 users, usage data analysis, and identification of critical workflows.

## Process

Progressive interface redesign prioritizing the most used features, with continuous validation through user testing.

## Solution

A modernized interface with keyboard shortcuts, intelligent auto-completion, and optimized forms.

## Results

Data entry time was reduced by 35% and user satisfaction increased by 52% according to post-deployment surveys.
